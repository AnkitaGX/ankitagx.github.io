About

My work focuses on statistical and econometric methods for dependent data, dynamic causal inference, and identification with imperfect or external instruments. I am particularly interested in settings where standard asymptotic tools become fragile and where theory can guide more reliable inference.

This website is a simple record of current work, drafts, and ongoing projects. It will grow as the papers do, which is a very academic way of saying it is alive and slightly unfinished.
